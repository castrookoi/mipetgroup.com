Core Service Areas

Mipet Services Limited is a leading provider of innovative solutions in the energy and public sectors. Our expertise spans the entire value chain, from the Upstream Sector, where we focus on exploration and production, to the Midstream Sector, emphasizing transportation and storage. We also excel in the Downstream Sector, offering refining and distribution services, and extend our capabilities to the Public Sector, delivering tailored consulting and infrastructure support to government and public entities. With a commitment to sustainability and efficiency, Mipet Services Limited ensures reliable and high-quality services across all these core areas.

Upstream Sector

In the Upstream Sector, Mipet Services Limited specializes in exploration, drilling, and production activities. We leverage advanced technologies to discover and extract resources efficiently, ensuring minimal environmental impact and maximum yield.

Midstream Sector

Our Midstream Sector services focus on transportation, storage, and processing of resources. Mipet Services Limited provides pipeline management, logistics, and infrastructure solutions to ensure safe and efficient movement of products.

Downstream Sector

In the Downstream Sector, Mipet Services Limited offers refining, marketing, and distribution services. We help transform raw materials into finished products and deliver them to end-users with a focus on quality and sustainability.

Public Sector

Mipet Services Limited supports the Public Sector with consulting, project management, and infrastructure development. We partner with government agencies to deliver efficient, compliant, and innovative solutions for public needs.
