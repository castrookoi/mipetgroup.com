Who we are

Who We Are

Mipet Services Limited (MSL) is a fully indigenous oil and gas services company, founded in the mid-1990s and incorporated in 2002 with 100% Nigerian ownership. Headquartered in Port Harcourt with facilities in Rivers and Delta States, MSL provides innovative, world-class solutions across the oil and gas value chain. Through strong alliances with international technical partners, we deliver safe, reliable, and cost-effective services that meet client requirements and industry standards. Our commitment to health, safety, and the environment has driven consistent growth and earned us a trusted reputation in Nigerias oil and gas sector.

What We Do

Mipet Services Limited delivers a wide range of specialized solutions across the upstream, midstream, and downstream sectors of the oil and gas industry, as well as the public sector. Our core expertise spans Technical Services, Infrastructure Development, Community Support Services, and Comprehensive Operational Support, covering the entire value chainExploration, Production, Transportation, Refining, and Distribution. Driven by a strong commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainability, we design and deliver tailored solutions that enhance operational efficiency, ensure reliability, and support long-term growth for our clients.
